So it starts

And so here it beings. I have had an interest in film photography for about five years now. I guess this post should be about how it all started and how I got to the stage of writing my first post for this new blog all about my experience with film and film cameras. I have been involved seriously in photography for about 6 years now and film photography has been one of the my favourite parts of photography and I feel that it is the part of my photography that has grown the fastest and continues to push me in my growth. My journey into film photography all happened by finding my Grandfathers Asahi Pentax SP in the back of the wardrobe. It had a couple of lenses and at the time I had a Fujifilm XE-1 and thought about using the lenses on that with an adaptor. After about 2 weeks of that I tried shooting my first roll of film and the obsession started from there.
